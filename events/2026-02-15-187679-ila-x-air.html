ILA X AIR

Sunday, February 15, 2026Kimball Arts Center1757 N Kimball Ave, Chicago, IL 60647 Opening Sunday, February 15th, from 3PM - 6PM

Winter AIR x ILA 2026 is a collaborative Artists in Residence exhibition presented by the Kimball Arts Center in partnership with ILA Creative Studio. On view from February through April 2026, A Good Look features new work by Winter AIR resident artists Gabi Bozeman, Zakia Rowlett, and Harlem West, whose practices engage contemporary portraiture, presence, and visual storytelling.

Join us for the opening reception on Sunday, February 15th from 3–6 PM in the Plant Lounge! An opportunity to meet the artists, experience the exhibition firsthand, collect orginal pieces and celebrate the Winter AIR cohort. The reception will include special drinks provided by:
