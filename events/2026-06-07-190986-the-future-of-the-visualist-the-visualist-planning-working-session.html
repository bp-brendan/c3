The Future of the Visualist— The Visualist Planning & Working Session

Sunday, June 7, 2026Spudnik Press1821 W Hubbard St, #203, Chicago, IL 60622 Opening Sunday, June 7th, from 10AM - 2:30PM

Join us for a collaborative working session as we map out the next phase of The Visualist. We will be breaking the day into three focused blocks to tackle our immediate operational, fundraising, and web development goals. Please feel free to join for the entire session or just the blocks that match your interests!

Schedule:

10:00 AM – 11:30 AM | Operations & Research: We’ll spend time doing hands-on research and data entry, followed by a discussion on how to streamline our workflow for future volunteer training.

11:30 AM – 12:30 PM | Fundraising & Organizational Stability: A session dedicated to reviewing our brand messaging, looking over past grant narratives, and brainstorming equitable fundraising ideas.

12:30 PM – 1:30 PM | Website Stabilization: We will review our current site framework and data mapping to plan out necessary upgrades.
