SWIPE LEFT

Friday, February 6, 2026Fulton Street Collective1821 W Hubbard St, Chicago, IL 60622 Opening Friday, February 6th, from 6PM - 10PM On view through Thursday, February 26th

Fulton Street Collective, in collaboration with West Town Chamber of Commerce, presents SWIPE LEFT Art Opening on Friday February 6th, from 6-10pm.

In an era of infinite choice, how do we process the finite sting of a broken heart?

“Swipe Left” explores the intersection of traditional romantic grief and the unique digital fatigue of modern-day dating. From the “ghosting” of the digital age to the timeless ache of a long-term parting, this exhibition seeks to map the landscape of heartbreak in 2026.

Curated by Jude Lo

To ensure the organic growth of West Town as Chicago’s emergent gallery district and further unite the neighborhood as a cultural destination, please join us in our gallery on the first Friday of every month for a collaborative art opening.

West Town Chamber of Commerce supports and elevates similar efforts while further facilitating connection among Chicago’s gallerists, artists, collectors and patrons.With enthusiastic participation from area galleries, First Fridays offers the potential to ensure the continued development of the West Town neighborhood as a nexus of fine art and arts development in Chicago and beyond.
