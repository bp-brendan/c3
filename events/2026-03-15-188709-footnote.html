*Footnote

Sunday, March 15, 2026Woman Made Gallery1332 S Halsted St, Chicago, IL 60607 Opening Sunday, March 15th, from 2PM - 4PM

Woman Made Gallery presents *Footnote, a literary event curated by Jae Green featuring long-form and experimental work that breaks from traditional short-form structures. Expect pieces that unfold through layered nuance, unconventional approaches, and a step-back perspective. *Footnote takes its name from that space at the bottom of the page, the note of reference, explanation, or comment that reframes everything above it. In that same spirit, this event moves beyond the constraints of traditional short-form poetry: the haiku, the epigram, the limerick, the sestina, and opens the floor to long-form and experimental work.

We’re honored to feature the voices of Cynthia Oka, Adrienne Nadeau, Naoko Fujimoto, Katherine Chronis, and Chirskira Caillouet.
