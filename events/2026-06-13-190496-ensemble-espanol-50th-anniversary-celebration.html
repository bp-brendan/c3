Ensemble Espanol: 50th Anniversary Celebration

Saturday, June 13, 2026, 7:30PM - 9:30PM North Shore Center for the Performing Arts in Skokie 9501 Skokie Blvd, Skokie, IL 60076 Official website

The world renowned Ensemble Español Spanish Dance Theater brings the passionate, expressive rhythms of Spain to life in honor of their 50th Anniversary Celebration for one performance only. Works include the world premiere of “Escenas Villanescas” by Ruiz, Dame Libby Komaiko Legacy Artistic Director Irma Suarez and popular works from the company’s repertoire including founder Komaiko’s iconic “Boléro”set to the music of Maurice Ravel. Tickets are $45 – $65 before ticketing and online purchasing fees. To purchase tickets and for more information visit NorthShoreCenter.org or call 847.673.6300.
