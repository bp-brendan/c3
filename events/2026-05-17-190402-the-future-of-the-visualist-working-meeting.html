The Future of The Visualist

Join us on May 17 for a day of collaborative working sessions focused on sustaining, strengthening, and shaping the future of The Visualist. These conversations are an opportunity for artists, organizers, contributors, and community members to share ideas, identify priorities, and help develop practical strategies for the platform’s long-term growth and stability. Participants are welcome to attend one session or stay for all three.

10:00–11:00 AM — Operations

A discussion focused on workflows, research and event-entry systems, volunteer coordination, and organizational processes that support the day-to-day function of The Visualist.

11:00 AM–12:00 PM — Fundraising and Organizational Stability

A working session addressing fundraising strategies, partnerships, financial sustainability, and the organizational support needed for long-term growth.

12:00–1:00 PM — Website Stabilization

A collaborative conversation centered on maintaining and improving the functionality, usability, and technical infrastructure of the website.
