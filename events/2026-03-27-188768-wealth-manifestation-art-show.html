Wealth Manifestation Art Show

Friday, March 27, 2026C.F.HQ Phigital Art Gallery1432 W Irving Park Rd, Chicago, IL 60613 Opening Friday, March 27th, from 6PM - 1130PM On view through Saturday, April 4th Official website

For the last three years, I have been focused on climbing out of the financial hole that COVID left me in. Every day, I have gone to the studio and painted the dollar sign. As I paint it, I meditate on how to bring wealth and abundance into my life. I keep painting until I begin to see the steps I need to take to make that happen. Sometimes that step is sending an email. Sometimes it is making a phone call. Sometimes it is creating a new advertising campaign. Once I complete that step, I return to the canvas and paint the dollar sign again, repeating the process over and over.

Through this practice, I finally dug myself out of the hole I found myself in. Now it is time to share these paintings with the world.

These works were created with intention, focus, and belief. My hope is not only to sell them, but to send them out into the world carrying the energy of wealth and abundance into other people’s homes. I think of my art as a kind of spell, a form of magic made visible through paint, repetition, and will.

Now I am ready to share that magic with you. I hope you become a collector of this work, and I hope it brings wealth, abundance, and possibility into your life.

Allen VandeverC.F.HQ Phigital Art GalleryChicagoWealth Manifestation Art Show
