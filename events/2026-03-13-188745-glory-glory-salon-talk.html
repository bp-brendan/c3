Glory! Glory! 🎉

Glory! Glory!, an exhibition and public programs centering Black artistic perspectives with both representational and interpretive images of the U.S. flag continues with a oderated conversation with artists, civic, and political leaders exploring symbolism, patriotism, protest, and personal narrative as they relate to the American flag and the nation’s evolving story.

Featured artists include Robert Lewis Clark, Nate Austin, Candace Hunter, Nnaemeka Ekwelum, and Paul Branton.
