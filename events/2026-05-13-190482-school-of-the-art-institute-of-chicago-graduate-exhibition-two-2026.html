School of the Art Institute of Chicago Graduate Exhibition Two 2026

Wednesday, May 13, 2026SAIC Galleries33 E Washington St, Chicago, IL 60602 Opening Wednesday, May 13th, from 5Pm - 7PM On view through Wednesday, May 20th Official website

Graduate Exhibition Two is the the culminating presentation of 98 MFA, MDDO, MArch, MArch-IA, and MSHP candidates in SAIC’s class of 2026, and an opportunity for them to present new and ambitious work to the public in the SAIC Galleries. Graduate Exhibition Two is the second of two graduate exhibitions this spring.
