Silhouettes Of A King :Heavy is the Head, Unseen is the Heart

Step into the shadows and light of Silhouettes of A King, an immersive exploration of modern black masculinity . This collection of works by Obisoulstar strips away the noise to reveal the raw, honest profiles of kings in our midst. From the towering strength required to lead, to the quiet paranoia of being watched, and the unseen weight of generational burdens, these silhouettes tell a story that is as regal as it is human.

Join us this Black History Month to witness the complexity behind the crown, sponsored by Moor’s Beer, a black owned craft beer company in Chicago.
