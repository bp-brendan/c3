Unordered List of Dreams — Spring 2026 Undergraduate Exhibition Public Closing Reception

Saturday, April 11, 2026SAIC Galleries33 E Washington St, Chicago, IL 60602 Opening Saturday, April 11th, from 12PM - 5PM On view through Saturday, April 11th

Come celebrate the culmination of years of hard work by 15 undergraduate artists at the closing reception of our student-organized exhibition! Unordered List of Dreams, is located in the SAIC Spring Undergraduate Exhibition. Students with a variety of practices have crafted works spanning from gigantic furniture, to floating suits and fields in miniature. Seemingly dissonant pieces have been woven together to create a fantastical, multimedia experience honoring each student’s artistic story. Come support the next generation of artists, curators, and writers!
