In Bloom

Saturday, January 31, 2026LVL31542 N Milwaukee Ave, #3, Chicago, IL 60622 Opening Saturday, January 31st, from 6PM - 9PM On view through Sunday, March 8th

LVL3 is excited to announce the opening of In Bloom, a five person exhibition celebrating the gallery’s 16th year anniversary, on view from January 31st to March 8th, 2026. In Bloom features the work of Zoe Alameda (Los Angeles, CA), Renata Berdes (Chicago, IL), Cathy Hsiao (Chicago, IL), AJ Kahn (Los Angeles, CA), and Tatiana Sky (Chicago, IL)

Another rotation around the sun, constantly spinning towards an inherent expansion. Unavoidably, a shedding pursues, revealing a new set of walls that persist toward the sky. Lore seeps out of the concrete cracks that exist only to crumble in a year’s time. Reflections breed new realities, and the crawling ivies recall what the new structure’s bones were made from. The inside is calm, loving. The outside is pulsing with hope, ready to begin again.

Opening Reception: January 31st, 6-9 PM
