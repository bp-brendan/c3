Friday, May 1, 2026The Palette & Chisel Academy of Fine Arts1012 N Dearborn St, Chicago, IL 60610 Opening Friday, May 1st, at 6PM On view through Monday, May 11th

Eight artists invite you to an exhibition of refined and personal work. Hosted in the historic gallery of the Palette & Chisel Academy of Fine Arts, this show is a celebration of craftsmanship, storytelling, and the beauty of representational art.

Stop by our Opening Reception on May 1st (6–9 PM) to celebrate with the artists and enjoy an evening of conversation alongside light refreshments.
