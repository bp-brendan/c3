‘if i were a blade of grass’ is a remote collaboration between SPACE 01 operator trejon d’angelo and interdisciplinary artist niya gao, a chinese canadian sculptor and installation artist currently living and working in china. the exhibition presents a selection of sculptures and sketches created by niya between 2022–2024, curated and installed by trejon at SPACE 01.
