Vibrance

Thursday, June 11, 2026Forum 301301 W Ohio St, Chicago, IL 60654 Opening Thursday, June 11th, from 6PM - 9PM On view through Saturday, June 20th

Vibrance is an exhibition illuminated by movement, identity, and the electric pulse of human connection. Featuring four Chicago based artists, photography, paintings, and neon glass sculptures transform the gallery into a living atmosphere, one saturated with color, rhythm, intimacy, and unapologetic presence. Presented during Pride Month, Vibrance serves as both celebration and declaration: an affirmation
