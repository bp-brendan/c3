MMM….marks Oliva Gallery’s ten-year anniversary with a group exhibition presented in conjunction with International Women’s Day. Featuring work by Melanie P. Brown, Mia Capodilupo, and Michelle Stone, the title references both the shared first initials of the participating artists and the visual and sensory richness of their work.
