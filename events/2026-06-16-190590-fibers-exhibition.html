Fibers Exhibition

Tuesday, June 16, 2026Side Practice Coffee5139 N Damen Ave, Chicago, IL 60625 Opening Tuesday, June 16th, from 5:30PM - 8PM

You are invited to the Fibers Exhibition, held at Side Practice Coffee. Fibers is an eight-portrait series that explores cultures once left behind, and how fabrics and garments preserve the memory of migration, survival, and beauty through photography. Each portrait weaves together visual storytelling and material symbolism, reflecting how identity is both preserved and transformed
