TRACES IN TIME

Friday, January 16, 2026Bridgeport Art Center1200 W 35th St, Chicago, IL 60609 Opening Friday, January 16th, from 7PM - 10PM On view through Friday, March 6th

Curated by Kelvin Larue Taylor

3RD FLOOR GALLERY

This exhibition focuses on works that explore memory, the marks we leave behind, and the passage of time.

Each piece creates a dialogue between permanence and transience, asking: What do we leave behind, and what do we carry forward? Together, the works form a canvas of time—an immersive space for reflection, memory, and discovery.
