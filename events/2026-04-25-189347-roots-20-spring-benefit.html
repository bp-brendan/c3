Roots & Culture turns 20 this year! We’d love to invite you to toast this milestone at our annual spring benefit!

We’re throwing ourselves the birthday party we want to have! Going back to our roots (see what we did there), kicking it old school, Chicago-style. Hotdogs, Italian beef (don’t worry vegans, we got you), beer and cocktails. On the sound system, we’re very stoked to welcome back our dearest friend, roommate #1, and original Double Exposure artist, Ryan Fenchel, spinning sweet reggae rhythms.

And of course, we’ve got a stacked auction roster featuring a who’s who of Roots alumni and recently exhibited artists. More info to be announced in the coming weeks!
