Pay What You Want Art Show

Friday, January 30, 2026Childhood Fractureds Phygital Art Gallery1432 W Irving Park Rd, Chicago, IL 60613 Opening Friday, January 30th, from 6PM - 11PM

I believe everyone deserves to have art on their walls.

That’s why I’m hosting a pay-what-you-can-afford art show.

If you’ve ever wanted to own one of my pieces, this is the easiest way to do it. Come through, find something that speaks to you, and pay what feels possible.

I’m hoping folks will pay what they can, plus a little extra if they feel the art is worth it. It’s all about sharing the love of art, after all.

But the fun doesn’t stop there! After 10PM, we’re diving into some figure drawing. Whether you want to be the model or the artist, it’s an evening of sensual creativity. Come join the party, take home some art, create something new, or inspire others. Let’s make art that’s as wild as you are!
