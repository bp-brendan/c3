College Arts Association: 2026 SAC Portfolio Review and Industry Advice for Artists

Friday, February 20, 2026Hilton Chicago, 2nd Floor - Boulevard.Hilton Chicago, 720 S Michigan Ave, Chicago, IL 60605 Opening Friday, February 20th, from 11AM - 12:30PM

We would like to invite your students to participate in the upcoming 2026 SAC Portfolio Review and Industry Advice for Artists , Friday, February 20, 2026, 11:00 AM – 12:30 PM hosted by Services to Artist Committee. This will take place as part of the College Arts Association annual conference at the Hilton Chicago, 720 S Michigan Ave, Chicago, IL 60605 in the Hilton Chicago – 2nd Floor – Boulevard. https://caa.confex.com/caa/2026/meetingapp.cgi/Session/16940

This opportunity provides an in-person platform to receive valuable input, advice, and critical feedback from leading professionals. Emerging, and established artists working in any medium, looking to forward their practice and build professional connections, are welcome to join. Please bring your portfolio, writing samples and questions.

You can access the reviewer’s area of expertise by clicking into here. https://docs.google.com/document/d/1i2hyWo8iZJtLcGzSy9VeyvEKpSZd8J0i7ouBfYztoFU/edit?tab=t.0

Click into the Portfolio Reviewer / Offering Industry Advice Form https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeukHNXeLfB0PDLxykqDJ9IlWTU0CjL3PxiJS9i9M_Jw_NUXA/viewform to reserve your session with a Portfolio Reviewer or Industry Advice Professional. A confirmation email with your assigned reviewer and session time will be sent to you prior to the conference. A sign-up sheet will also be available during the event for any remaining openings.

You must register for the CAA conference but we offer no-cost registration which includes access to conference key events (Convocation, Annual Artist Interviews, Distinguished Scholar Session), CAA Committee and Affiliated Society Business Meetings, Workshops, Services to Artists Committee (SAC) programming and Student and Emerging Professionals Committee (SEPC) programming. Please note this no-cost registration permits access to only these programs and events. You can register here: Annual Conference | Programs | CAA.
