Nov 5th 2020

Intersect Chicago

@ Virtual Fair (Formerly SOFA )

Online

Opening Thursday, November 5th, from 12PM - 5PM

On view through Thursday, November 12th

« »

Intersect Chicago is replacing SOFA virtually for the 2020 art fair edition due to COVID-19. Live online from November 6-12, with a dedicated VIP Preview day on November 5. The fair will feature a special focus for each day, including: Glass, Contemporary Art, Ceramic and Craft, Design, Outsider Art, Fiber Art, and Public Art / Sculpture.
