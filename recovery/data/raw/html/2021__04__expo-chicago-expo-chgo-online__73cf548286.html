EXPO CHICAGO: EXPO CHGO ONLINE

@ EXPO CHICAGO

On view through Monday, April 12th

« »

EXPO CHGO ONLINE | April 8 – 12, 2021 isa curated digital exposition of contemporary and modern art complemented by five days of virtual programming, presented in collaboration with Hook. EXPO CHGO ONLINE features over 80 galleries in a focused online presentation, with a portion of proceeds benefiting the Art Dealers Association of America (ADAA).

EXPO CHGO ONLINE maintains our commitment to rigorous programming and curatorial initiatives presenting select programming that highlights artist exhibitions, screenings, and performances in partnership with our network of local, national and international institutions, museums, and non-profits. A portion of the programming evolves from the environmentally focusedAlternate Assembly: Environmental Impact in the Era of Pandemicsymposium launched January 21-23, 2021.

EXHIBITION WEEKEND

For visitors or Chicagoans looking for an art-filled staycation weekend, EXPO CHICAGO is collaborating with Newcity to offer select hotel packages and opportunities for patrons to explore Chicago’s leading art galleries and institutions. EXHIBITION Weekend will take place Friday, April 9th and Saturday, April 10th.  Click here to learn more.

Streaming from April 8 – 12
