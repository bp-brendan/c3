May 31st 2019

The Evanston Made Group Show

@ Evanston Art Center

1717 Central St, Evanston, IL 60201

Opening Friday, May 31st, from 6PM - 10PM

On view through Sunday, June 30th

« »

Evanston artists living or working in Evanston are invited to participate in the 6th Annual Evanston Made Group Show at the Evanston Arts Center, May 31-June 30, 2019 at the Evanston Art Center.

Opening Party is May 31, 6-10p, we’ll screen the SIDE/Lot Experimental Films at 8:45p in the parking lot.

See a slide show of group show imges here https://evanstonmade.org/group-show-artists-2019/
