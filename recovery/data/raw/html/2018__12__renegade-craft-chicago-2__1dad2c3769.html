Renegade Craft Chicago

@ Bridgeport Art Center

1200 W 35th St, Chicago, IL 60609

Opening Saturday, December 1st, from 11AM - 5PM

On view through Sunday, December 2nd

« »

We’re coming home to Chicago’s Bridgeport Art Center with a festive weekend of contemporary handmade ✌︎ Warm up with a seasonal cocktail while shopping small from 200+ of the very best emerging and established Makers, grooving to Reckless Records and Lumpen Radio DJ sets, snacking on local bites, workshopping, and so much more. See you there!

~ Renegade is free to attend and all are welcome ~
