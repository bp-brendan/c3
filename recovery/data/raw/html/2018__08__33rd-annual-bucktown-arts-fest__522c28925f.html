33rd Annual Bucktown Arts Fest

@ Bucktown Arts Fest

2200 N Oakley Ave, Chicago, IL 60647

Opening Saturday, August 25th, at 11AM

On view through Sunday, August 26th

« »

The fest of fests! By the community for the community!

200 artists, craft beer, sangria, food trucks, two stages, countless performers, fun kids stuff – and all proceeds go to fund arts education in the neighborhood. (No really, all proceeds… we have no paid staff). See you there!

FREE ADMISSION, NO TICKETS NEEDED
