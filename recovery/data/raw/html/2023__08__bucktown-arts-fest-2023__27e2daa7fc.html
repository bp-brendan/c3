Aug 26th 2023

Bucktown Arts Fest 2023

@ Bucktown Arts Fest

2200 N Oakley Ave, Chicago, IL 60647

Opening Saturday, August 26th, from 11AM - 7PM

On view through Sunday, August 27th

« »

Join us for the Bucktown Arts Fest 2023! Free to the public. Close to 200 artists representing their own work, our main stage and jazz stage, plus beer, food trucks, neighbors, love and so much more.
A fest by the community, for the community – all proceeds from the volunteer-run Bucktown Arts Fest go to fund, develop and support arts and education programming at Holstein Park and in local Bucktown/Wicker Park classrooms.
Learn more at www.bucktownartsfest.com
