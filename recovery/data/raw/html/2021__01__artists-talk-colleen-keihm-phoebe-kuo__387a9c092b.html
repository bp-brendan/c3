ARTISTS TALK: Colleen Keihm & Phoebe Kuo

@ FLXST Contemporary

Online

Opening Saturday, January 16th, from 1PM - 2:30PM

On view through Sunday, January 24th

« »

Join us for our first joint artist talk of 2021, with photographer Colleen Keihm & woodworker Phoebe Kuo. The artists will talk about their current exhibition IN THIS SYSTEM at FLXST Contemporary, their process, their intent, their thoughts about making art during the COVID pandemic, and more. The conversation will be moderated by FLXST Contemporary gallery director Jan Christian Bernabe.

WHAT: IN THIS SYSTEM: Artists Talk with Colleen Keihm & Phoebe Kuo

WHEN: Saturday, January 16, 2021: 1-2:30 pm

HOW: via ZOOM

All are welcome to join the conversation!
