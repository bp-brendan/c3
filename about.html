About

The Visualist is Chicago’s visual arts calendar — a week-to-week guide to exhibitions, talks, performances, and openings across Chicagoland, and a collective document of the artists, makers, and curators who do the city’s cultural work.

Our submit button is your submit button.

The Visualist follows On The Make, a calendar project developed by Karly Wildenhaus and re-imagined by Jenny Kendler, Chad Kouri, and Steve Ruiz. It is administered by culture/Math, an artist-run 501(c)(3) that develops tools to support Chicago’s cultural community. Questions or suggestions: Visualistchicago@gmail.com.

The Visualist is a 2016 recipient of the Propeller Grant. culture/Math is the recipient of a 2020 CityArts Grant and is partially supported by the Builders Initiative and the Illinois Arts Council Agency.
